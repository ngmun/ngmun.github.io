NGMUN V

Sunday, April 16, 2023

JCC: The Romanovs

Corban Shih

Chair

cshih24@nobles.edu

Corban is a junior at the Noble and Greenough School, and has been participating in Model UN since his freshman year. He revels in the competitive problem-solving associated with MUN, and he especially loves the added complexity and intimacy of Crisis Committees. He loves all things history, and is beyond excited to rewrite events of the past and act as Chair of the Joint Crisis Committee (JCC): The Romanovs! When Corban's not scribbling letters to the backroom, he can be found captaining Nobles' debate team, playing golf, or reviewing a new ramen restaurant.

Kate Wei

Co-Chair

kwei24@nobles.edu

Kate is a junior at the Noble and Greenough School and began attending Model UN conferences as a freshman. She is excited for a fun conference full of engaging debate and negotiation and can't wait to see everyone in committee! Outside of MUN, Kate participates in debate and Science Olympiad, runs cross country, and rows for the crew team.
